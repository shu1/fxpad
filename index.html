DJ effects pad

Click anywhere in pad to start playing, once track names appear at bottom.
Click/drag circle to change effects. See filter usage below.
Click on track names to toggle effects for each track.
Enter a soundcloud url and hit enter to play soundcloud track.
Load up to 10 of your own files at once to play them simultaneously.

Low Pass Filter - Drag mouse horizontally in left half of screen.
High Pass Filter - Drag mouse horizontally in right half of screen.
Resonance - Drag mouse vertically in top or bottom half of screen.

Made using Web Audio API.
