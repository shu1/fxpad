DJ effects pad

Tap anywhere in pad to start playing, once track names appear at bottom.
Tap/drag circle to change effects. See filter usage below.
Tap on track names to toggle its effects, or hit the number keys on your keyboard.
Enter a soundcloud url and hit enter key to play soundcloud track.
Load up to 10 of your own files at once to play them.

Low Pass Filter - Drag circle horizontally in left half of pad.
High Pass Filter - Drag circle horizontally in right half of pad.
Resonance - Drag circle vertically in top or bottom half of pad.

Made using Web Audio API.
